About Us

Who We Are

The Waypoint Amateur Radio Club is a community of amateur radio operators in Olathe and south Johnson County dedicated to growing the hobby. We support our members through hands-on learning, shared resources, and a welcoming space for operators of all experience levels to connect and get on the air.
