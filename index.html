VE Testing

The Waypoint Amateur Radio Club hosts Volunteer Examiner (VE) testing sessions for new and upgrading amateur radio operators. Our VE team is accredited with both the Laurel VEC and the Central Alabama VEC (CAVEC).

  • Laurel VEC Team Lead: Derek Rowland — NZ0P
  • CAVEC Team Lead: Matt Robertson — KD0WGZ
